Beef stew

Beef stew is a classic comfort food that is perfect for a cold day. It is a hearty dish made with beef, vegetables, and broth. This recipe makes 6 servings.

Ingredients

  • Beef chuck roast
  • Carrots and potatoes
  • Onion and garlic
  • Beef broth
  • Tomato paste
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Season the beef with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the beef and cook until browned on all sides. Remove beef and set aside.
  2. Add the onion and garlic to the pot and cook until softened. Add the tomato paste and flour, and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Return the beef to the pot and add the beef broth, carrots, and potatoes. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 1 1/2 hours or until beef is tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy!
