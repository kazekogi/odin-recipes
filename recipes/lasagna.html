Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is a family favorite. It is a layered pasta dish with meat, cheese and tomato sauce. This recipe makes 12 servings.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta and mozzarella cheese
  • Italian sausage and ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Egg
  • Onion and garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  3. Spread 1 cup of sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer with 1/3 of the noodles, the ricotta mixture, and the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers twice, ending with mozzarella.
  4. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray, or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
